Maine Lobster

Maine lobsters are known worldwide for their sweet and tender meat. Don't miss the lobster rolls at the festival!

Maine Blueberries

Wild blueberries from Maine are smaller but richer in flavor than their larger cousins. Try some blueberry pie!

Whoopie Pies

A beloved Maine treat, whoopie pies consist of two soft cookies with a fluffy filling. A must-try!
